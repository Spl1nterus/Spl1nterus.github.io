Product Details

Universal Premium Rubber Floor Mats by Michelin®. Most drivers need full floor coverage on their cars because driving can be messy business. Michelin's unique design on all-weather universal mat provides the deep channels and DirtTraps you'd expect from a much pricier custom mat, with Flexlines that bend and fold to meet the shape of your floor. If you see areas where a fit can be more precise, simply trim the outer edge with scissors for a more custom look.

Michelin's unique design on all-weather universal mat provides the deep channels and DirtTraps you'd expect from a much pricier custom mat, with Flexlines that bend and fold to meet the shape of your floor.

Specifications

  • Driver Mat: 28.5” L x 20” W x 0.4” H (72.4cm x 50.8cm x 1cm)
  • Passenger Mat: 28.3” L x 20.4” W x 0.4” H (71.8cm x 51.8cm x 1cm)
  • Rear: 14.3” L x 18.3” W x 0.4” H (36.2cm x 46.4cm x 1cm)

Features

  • Premium carpet and rubber is naturally soil and stain resistant
  • Form Fit Flexlines™ - Contours to every interior
  • Dirt Traps™ - Collect dirt, water and loose debris
  • Tread & Cleat™ backing holds mats firmly in place
  • Trim lines designed to provide a custom fit
  • Universal fit mats designed to fit more vehicles including cars, trucks, vans and SUV’s
  • Easy to clean with soap and water
